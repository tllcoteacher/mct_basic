How It Works

  1. Elicit: Student solves a problem. AI elicits the student’s reasoning, not just their answers.
  2. Log: the entire interaction as training data, utterances, inferred state, chosen teaching move, and outcome as training data.
  3. Diagnose: AI maps the reasoning onto a detailed cognitive learning trajectory: Ex: “Accurate visual fraction arithmetic, accurate multiplicative justification, fragile on formal symbolic identity, level 5”
  4. Learn: Every interaction trains the system. Framework gets sharper. Instruction gets tighter.
  5. Teach: Meet the learner exactly where they are developmentally with the best lesson, explanation, worked example, and practice problems to move them up the stack.

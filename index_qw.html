Demo

How it works in 90 seconds:

  1. Elicit: Student solves a problem on a digital whiteboard. AI asks: "How did you think about that?"
  2. Diagnose: AI maps the transcript to our framework: "Additive thinking, counting strategies, fragile on unit coordination."
  3. Intervene: AI selects the perfect nudge: a visual model, a counter-example, or a strategic question—not a hint.
  4. Learn: Every interaction trains the system. Framework gets sharper. Instruction gets tighter.

Result: Misconceptions get repaired *before* they calcify. Teachers get superpowers. Data becomes a moat.
